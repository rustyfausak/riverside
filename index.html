Riverside Ultimate is a mens club ultimate team from Austin, TX. We compete in the South Central region, Texas section of the USAU open club division.

About Us

Riverside Ultimate is the oldest team that still competes from Austin, TX. The team originated as a group of pickup players that first met in 1989. Riverside's first tournament play was in 1995.
